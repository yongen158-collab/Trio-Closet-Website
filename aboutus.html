About TRIO CLOSET

We are a boutique fashion store offering curated collections for men, women and children. Our focus is on premium feel, affordable pricing and excellent customer service — made with care in Malaysia.

Since our beginning, we've been dedicated to bringing thoughtful design, quality materials and great value to everyday wardrobes.

Our Mission

To provide stylish, durable clothing and accessories while keeping sustainability and customer service at the forefront. We want our customers to feel confident and comfortable in every piece.

Our Story

2020 — Founded

Trio Closet began as a small online shop with a passion for well-made basics.

2021 — First Pop-up

We opened our first pop-up store and met our community in person.

2023 — Expanded Range

Introduced kids and accessories collections, and improved sustainability standards.

2025 — New HQ

Moved operations to Johor Bahru with a dedicated team and improved logistics.
